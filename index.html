// How it works

Three Steps to Magic

01

📷

Camera Captures

OpenCV captures your webcam feed in real time, frame by frame.

02

🖐️

Hand Detected

MediaPipe detects your hand and tracks 21 landmark points with high accuracy.

03

📏

Distance Measured

The distance between your thumb and index finger is calculated precisely.

04

🔊

Volume Changes

pycaw maps the distance directly to your system volume — smooth and instant.

// Gestures

Control with Your Hand

👐

Fingers Apart

Spread your thumb and index finger wide to increase the volume.

🤏

Fingers Close

Bring your thumb and index finger together to decrease the volume.

⌨️

Press Q

Press Q on your keyboard to quit the application anytime.
